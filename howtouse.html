  • How to use

Step 1. Go to Netflix.com and find a show, then click on red arrow once hovering the show you want to watch

Step 2. From the address bar copy the numbers for the show you selected

Step 3. Paste the numbers copied from step 2 into the textbox which states "Netflix ID" and click the button next to it. Enjoy!
